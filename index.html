About Me

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate in Department of Computer Science at University of California, Irvine advised by Prof. Alfred Chen. My research interests are CPS/IoT autonomy software security and machine learning security, especially in the context of autonomous driving systems.

Before joining UCI, I received my M.S. and B.E. degree from Lehigh University and Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications. During my time at Lehigh University, I was fortunate to work with Prof. Ting Wang, Prof. Yinzhi Cao, and Prof. Mooi Choo Chuah.
