About Me

I am a fifth-year Ph.D. candidate in Department of Computer Science at University of California, Irvine advised by Prof. Alfred Chen. My research focuses on security aspects of safety-critical Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS), including the security of Autonomous Driving (AD) and robotics systems, as well as machine learning security.

Before joining UCI, I received my M.S. and B.E. degree from Lehigh University and Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications. During my time at Lehigh University, I was fortunate to work with Prof. Ting Wang, Prof. Yinzhi Cao, and Prof. Mooi Choo Chuah.
